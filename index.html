    Guidance

    Focus: Providing information, suggestions, or direction to help someone make decisions (e.g., career, education, lifestyle).
    Nature: Preventive and developmental.
    Relationship: Not very deep; often short-term.
    Example: A student asking for career options after graduation.

    Counselling

    Focus: Helping individuals explore and resolve emotional, social, or personal issues through dialogue.
    Nature: Supportive and collaborative; insight-oriented.
    Relationship: Requires trust and rapport; short- to mid-term.
    Example: Helping someone manage stress, relationship issues, or low self-esteem.

    Therapy

    Focus: Treating psychological disorders, trauma, or deeply rooted emotional struggles using specialized therapeutic approaches.
    Nature: Clinical, diagnostic, often long-term.
    Relationship: Deep, structured, and guided by trained mental health professionals.
    Example: Cognitive Behavioral Therapy for anxiety or depression.

    Training

    Focus: Teaching specific skills or competencies through structured programs.
    Nature: Practical, goal-oriented, and usually group-based.
    Relationship: Instructor–participant dynamic.
    Example: Assertiveness training, emotional intelligence workshops, communication skills sessions.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Common questions about The Self Care Lab and our reflective counseling approach

    How long is a counselling session?

    Standard sessions are 60 minutes. Shorter 15–30 minute consults or follow-ups are also available depending on the service selected.

    Do you offer online and in-person sessions?

    Yes — we provide both video consultations and in-person sessions. You can choose your preferred format when booking.

    Where are your in-person sessions held?

    In-person appointments are held at our clinic location listed on the Contact page. Exact directions are provided after booking.

    What are your session fees and package options?

    We publish current fees and packages on the Pricing section — common options include single sessions, the Self Care Pack (3 sessions) and a Monthly Mentorship plan. Contact us for custom packages.

    What kind of issues do you help with?

    We help with stress, burnout, anxiety, relationship concerns, identity and life transitions, self-esteem, and goal-focused growth work.

    What counselling techniques do you use?

    We draw from evidence-based approaches such as CBT-informed techniques, mindfulness, behavioral activation, and practical reflective exercises tailored to each client.

    What is your counselling approach or philosophy?

    Our approach emphasizes practical self-care systems, client collaboration, and short-term actionable steps that build long-term resilience and insight.

    Is my information kept confidential?

    Yes. All communications and records are treated as confidential following professional ethics and data protection standards. We only share information with consent or as legally required.

    Do children need parental consent for testing?

    Yes. For psychological testing or formal assessments, parental or guardian consent is required for minors. We provide clear consent forms and explain the process before testing.

    Will I get a progress or assessment report?

    Where relevant (e.g., assessments or structured packages) we provide written summaries or progress reports. These are shared securely and discussed in-session.

    How can I book a session?

    Use the Book / Schedule link on the site to open our scheduling page, or contact us by phone or email and we’ll help you find a convenient time.

    What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept cards, UPI and major online payment methods via our payment provider. Specific options are shown at checkout.

    Do you offer home visits for children’s vocational counselling?

    Occasionally we can arrange home or school visits for vocational assessment depending on the case and logistics. Please contact us to discuss availability and fees.

    Do you provide corporate wellness programs?

    Yes — we offer customized corporate workshops, training and employee wellness programs. Contact us for a tailored proposal.

    Do you host mental health awareness events or workshops?

    We regularly run workshops and awareness sessions. Check our Events/Resources section or contact us to enquire about upcoming sessions.
